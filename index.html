Scotland

Scotland is a country that was founded sometime in the 9th century.

Originally, before they spoke english, the Scots spoke gaelic. More specifically, they spoke scottish gaelic. The population today is 5.563 million people.

The scottish land is most northerly of the four parts of the United Kingdom.
The name comes from the Latin Scotia, Land of the Scots, which are Celtic people from Ireland who settled on the west coast of Great Britain.

Scotland, like Ireland, has many castle ruins that are scattered around the land. There are many tours that go around scotland viewing them.
